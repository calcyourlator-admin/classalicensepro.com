Useful Tip-The "Home Improvement Trap"

Many people seeking to become licensed contractors settle for a Home Improvement (HIC) specialty because it's easier to obtain based on limited trade knowledge requirements. However, it may still take a long time to be approved to test for and obtain. HIC also severely restricts the scope of work a contractor can legally perform.
